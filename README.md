  ## About ItemTracker AI

  ItemTracker AI is a comprehensive household inventory management app that helps you:

  - Track items throughout your home
  - Organize belongings by rooms and containers
  - Use QR codes for quick item identification
  - Generate detailed inventory reports
  - Manage household members and shared access
  - Set up automated notifications and reminders

  ## Features

  ### Core Features

  - **Room Management:** Organize your home by rooms and spaces
  - **Container Tracking:** Track items within specific containers, boxes, or storage areas
  - **QR Code Generation:** Create QR codes for containers and items for quick scanning
  - **Check-in/Check-out System:** Monitor when items are borrowed or moved
  - **Search & Filter:** Quickly find specific items with powerful search tools
  - **Photo Documentation:** Add photos to visually identify your items
  - **AI Detection:** Use AI to automatically identify and catalog items from photos

  ### Premium Features (Subscription Required)

  - **Cloud Sync:** Access your inventory across multiple devices
  - **Household Sharing:** Share inventory access with family members
  - **Unlimited Items:** No limits on rooms, containers, or items
  - **Multiple Photos:** Add multiple photos per item
  - **Export Data:** Export inventory data to CSV, Excel, or JSON formats
  - **AI Assistant:** Get help organizing with AI-powered suggestions
  - **Priority Support:** Faster response times for technical issues

  ## Getting Started

  1. **Download the App:** Install ItemTracker AI from the App Store
  2. **Create Account:** Sign up with your email, Google account, or Sign in with Apple
  3. **Set Up Rooms:** Create rooms that match your home layout
  4. **Add Containers:** Add storage units, cabinets, boxes, etc. to your rooms
  5. **Add Items:** Start adding items to your inventory

  ## Common Issues

  ### Login Problems

  - Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  - Try signing out and signing back in
  - Check that your email/password are correct
  - For Google sign-in issues, make sure Google account permissions are enabled
  - For Apple sign-in issues, ensure Sign in with Apple is enabled in your Apple ID settings

  ### Sync Issues

  - Verify you have an active Premium subscription for cloud sync
  - Check your internet connection
  - Try manually syncing by pulling down on the main screen
  - Sign out and back in to refresh your account

  ### Missing Items or Data

  - Check if you're looking in the correct room or container
  - Use the search function to locate items
  - Verify you're signed into the correct account
  - For cloud sync users, ensure your subscription is active

  ## Subscription Information

  ItemTracker AI offers two subscription tiers:

  ### Monthly Subscription
  - Full access to all premium features
  - Cloud sync across devices
  - Household sharing capabilities
  - Priority support

  ### Yearly Subscription
  - All monthly features included
  - Significant cost savings vs monthly
  - Same premium feature access

  ### Managing Your Subscription

  - Subscriptions are managed through your App Store account
  - To cancel: Go to iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > ItemTracker AI
  - Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period
  - Data remains accessible during subscription period

  ## Troubleshooting

  ### App Crashes or Won't Open

  - Force close the app and reopen
  - Restart your device
  - Update to the latest app version
  - If issues persist, contact support

  ### Performance Issues

  - Close other apps running in background
  - Ensure you have sufficient storage space
  - Check for app updates
  - Restart your device

  ### Export/Import Issues

  - Ensure you have sufficient storage space
  - Check file permissions
  - Try exporting smaller batches of data
  - Contact support for large data exports
